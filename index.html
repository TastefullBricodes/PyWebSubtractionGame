FAQ
01
Who are we?

The History Club is a society of students that want to enrich themselves in knowledge of Indonesian and Global History while familiarizing themselves with political and social concepts and develop soft skills in conveying arguments, debating, and presenting them via media.

02
What are out goals?

Our goal is to create well rounded and skilled students who are fluent in communication in terms of soft skills while equipped with knowledge in Sociology, History, Politics, and Ethnology.

03
What Do We Do?

We encourage our students to do research on historical topics and present them in the form of live presentations, blogs, or debates on certain topics that involve historical phenomenons or trends
